About Me

I’m a Data & Business Intelligence Practitioner who enjoys working across analysis, engineering, and strategy. From writing SQL queries to building ETL pipelines and dashboards, I’m comfortable cleaning messy datasets, improving pipelines, and creating visuals that highlight the numbers that matter. I like working where messy business problems meet technical solutions, finding ways to bring structure and clarity to complex questions.

Outside of analytics, I enjoy experimenting in the kitchen, where building a recipe often feels like building a data model: both start messy, require balance and creativity, and when done right, become more than the sum of their parts. To recharge, I love traveling to catch new sunsets — a reminder that, much like in data, fresh perspectives can completely change the picture.
